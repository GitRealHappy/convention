The Workshop Series

8 Weeks of FREE Workshops to Gain a "Minimum Viable Skill Stack" for the Future of Your Creative Entrepreneurship, plus a community of fellow seekers to grow with.

Week 1 — Saturday, March 7th @ 11am PST
Dan Koe

Creating Your AI Powered Content System

If you feel the quiet pull toward something bigger than your job title, this one is for you. Dan Koe has built one of the most distinctive and philosophically rich voices in the creator economy around a simple but radical idea: your work and your life shouldn’t be separate. He’ll be breaking down how you can use AI intelligently to create a content system driven by your unique point of view.

Week 2 — Saturday, March 14th @ 11am PST
Taylin John Simmonds

How to Produce Writing that Moves People

Taylin Simmonds built a ghostwriting agency that has generated over 2 billion impressions. He’s had half-million-dollar years as a ghostwriter. And he’s a multi-passionate creator himself, which is exactly why he’s the right person to help you get clear on how to produce writing that moves people.

Week 3 — Saturday, March 21st @ 11am PST
David Morin

Using Writing to Move Yourself: Getting In Tune With Your Inner Voice

David's work with poetry and existential guidance will help dissolve the blocks to your inner voice, but also deepen your self expression, motive power, and vocational clarity.

Week 4 — Saturday, March 28th @ 11am PST
Kimia Nora

Rewire Your Brain, Rewire Your Business

Your habits, beliefs, and patterns aren't fixed. They're neurological programs. Kimia Nora brings the science of how your brain actually changes, and what you can do to direct that change intentionally. This session bridges neuroscience and personal transformation, giving you practical tools to stop running old programs and start building new ones.

Week 5 — Saturday, April 4th @ 11am PST
Hussain Ibarra

Attracting an Attentive Audience

You can have the vision, the offer, and the inner work done, but if no one knows you exist, none of it matters. Hussain Ibarra is a master of execution when it comes to growing an audience who genuinely cares about what you do. This session is about showing up, being found, and building the kind of following that lasts.

Week 6 — Saturday, April 11th @ 11am PST
Logan Quinn

Film School for Creators

Video is the most powerful medium available to creators today, and most people barely know how to stitch together a reel in Capcut. Logan Quinn brings a filmmaker's eye to the creator world, teaching you the craft behind making video content that's not just watchable, but genuinely good.

Week 7 — Saturday, April 18th @ 11am PST
Jack Moses

The River of Money

Once the foundation is built, it's time to level up. Jack Moses brings a perspective on money, value, and abundance that goes beyond tactics, helping you understand what it actually takes to step into a new tier of what's possible for your business, your clients, and your life.

Week 8 — Saturday, April 25th @ 11am PST
Dan Goldfield

Effortless Effectiveness

Dan Goldfield brings a Dharma-rooted teaching on effortless productivity, how to do your best work not through grinding, but through flow, presence, and removing the buggy software that robs your peace of mind.

The Grand Prize

One winner will receive:

  • 2 years of full access membership in the Living Internet University, beginning June 2026 ($480 value)
  • 2 virtual tickets to the Future-Proof Creator Summit, or 1 GA ticket if you can attend in person (hotel not included) ($300 value)

  • A custom wooden wallet from Oakmoss.ca, shipped anywhere for free ($200 value)

How It Works

  • Earn entries: Each workshop you attend and document = one entry in the draw.
  • Create your post: A video where you speak, an article/letter/thread, or artwork with text explaining how it applies what you learned.
  • Past workshops count: Recordings are available, so you can still enter for workshops that already happened.
  • One entry is enough: Even attending one workshop and making one post makes you eligible to win.

How to Enter

  1. Join the Community: Sign up for the Living Internet University's free virtual community.
  2. Watch a Workshop: Attend live or watch the recording.
  3. Document What You Learned: Create and publish your post on any social platform.
  4. Submit Your Entry: Share the link in the "Transformation Contest" section of the community.
