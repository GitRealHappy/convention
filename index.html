WHY MOST CREATORS STAY STUCK

You're Not Stuck Because You're Lazy. You're Stuck Because The Playbook Is Shallow.

"I know my work matters, but my message still doesn't cut through."
"I keep switching tactics, but none feel sustainable for my energy or life."
"My audience growth is inconsistent, and revenue feels unpredictable month to month."
"I can publish, but I've lost touch with my inner voice and what I actually stand for."

Most creators are trained to chase attention before they build identity, standards, and systems.

So the same cycle repeats: new tactic, short spike, emotional crash, restart. You work hard, but your business still feels fragile.

It doesn't only cost growth. It costs confidence, consistency, and cash flow.

At some point, you realize this is bigger than content strategy. It is about the kind of work you are building your life around: job, career, or calling.

When strategy is disconnected from identity, every win feels temporary.

The creators who build resilient businesses do not just optimize posts. They evolve how they think, how they position their work, and how they solve meaningful problems for specific people.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this event for?

Creator-entrepreneurs, coaches, consultants, educators, and practitioners who want to grow revenue while building work that still feels aligned and meaningful.

How long is the event and what's the schedule?

It's a 3-day event starting Friday, June 5 at 1:00PM and ending Sunday, June 7 at 4:30PM. You'll get talks, workshops, curated networking, and implementation planning blocks.

Where is the event held?

At the Sheraton Vancouver Airport Hotel. Ticket holders will receive maps, travel details, and local recommendations. The venue includes a free YVR shuttle and easy transit access.

Are there nearby hotels and special rates?

Yes. We have attendee rates at the Sheraton (host hotel) and the Marriott next door. Details are sent after ticket purchase. Rate deadlines apply, so booking early is recommended.

What is the refund and transfer policy?

Tickets are non-refundable and transferable until May 1st, 2026.

Will we be able to meet speakers?

Some speakers will have a Q& after their talks. They may be present for informal interaction windows as well. VIP attendees also get an intimate Saturday dinner with speakers and peers.

Do virtual attendees get recordings?

Yes. Virtual tickets include livestream access plus 12-month replay access so you can review key sessions on your own schedule.

Is there a payment plan?

No. Payment plans are not available at this time.
