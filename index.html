Claim Your Seat

The world has gone mechanical.

As machine learning advances, the humanity that once filled the web with life has been flattened.

The internet was supposed to set us free. Instead, it has turned many of us into machines pretending to be human.

Yet, here we are at a precipice: According to Forbes, the Creator Economy is on track to surpass $480 billion by 2027.

The opportunity is undeniable. The challenge is that most creators are not equipped to navigate the landscape of the Creator Economy without becoming burnt out and disillusioned.

This gathering exists because there are enough webinars... you need a more immersive catalyst to truly initiate your quest for meaningful creation.

A weekend to rediscover what makes you human, the skills to build a powerful brand around it, and the community to support you on your journey.

What it's all about:

Find the others who are serious about finding their way, immerse yourself among those who are ahead of you, and inject yourself into a tribe.

Three days to transform the trajectory of your creative work, your social development, and your financial future.

With hundreds of creators together in the brilliant Brittannia Ballroom at the Sheraton Vancouver Airport Hotel, you'll have the opportunity to connect with the people who are building the future of the creator economy.

This is a gathering for creators who are serious about building value, and who are willing to invest in themselves and their future.

By creators, for creators; The Living Internet Alliance seeks to address the gaping holes in the Creator Economy's future.

Surely you know of the "dead internet theory," that online traffic and content is on route to be almost entirely driven and generatedby bots and AI.

Through curated online communities and bonds cured in the kiln of in-person events, you can stay tuned to the signal amidst the sea of noise.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this event for?

The Creator Economy Convention is for creators of all kinds — writers, artists, filmmakers, podcasters, YouTubers, coaches, freelancers, entrepreneurs — anyone building their work online and ready to do it with depth. We embrace AI as a tool, but we believe real connection and originality come from people.

  • Curated Keynote Presentations: Learn directly from creators who’ve built thriving one-person businesses and movements.
  • VIP Experiences: Premium dinner and exclusive settings for deeper, more meaningful conversations with headline speakers.
  • Hands-on Workshops: Practical, interactive sessions with strategies you can implement immediately.
  • Vibrant International Community: Join a diverse global network building the future together.
  • Exclusive Networking Opportunities: Connect with ambitious creators and potential collaborators.
  • Pre-Convention Circle Community: Get access upon ticket purchase to coordinate travel, hotels, and meet attendees before Vancouver.
Where will this event be held?

The event will be held at the Sheraton Vancouver Airport Hotel. Full details — maps, transportation tips, and local recommendations — will be sent to ticket holders and shared in our private Circle community. For its guests, the hotel offers a free shuttle from YVR, a 24-hr gym, and a heated pool.

How long is the event and what’s the schedule like?

It’s a 3-day event starting Friday, June 5 at 1:00PM and ending Sunday, June 7 at 4:30PM.

We’ll also have community virtual meetups before and after the weekend to keep momentum strong. A full schedule will be released closer to the date. Plan for inspiring days punctuated by short breaks for rest and conversation.

The after-party will be held on Sunday night at 8:00PM at a private venue.

Is The Creator Economy Convention an in-person or a virtual event?

This is primarily an in-person gathering — because real chemistry can’t be downloaded. For those who can’t travel, we plan to offer a livestream of main-stage talks. More details about livestream tickets will be released in 2026. Recordings will be available to all ticket-holders after the event.

Are tickets to the Sunday after-party included?

After-party tickets are sold separately (but are included with VIP tickets). Announcements will be posted in the Circle community for ticket-holders with details on location and capacity. It’s our closing celebration — where new friendships become lasting collaborations.

Are there nearby hotels?

Yes! We’ve arranged special group rates at the Sheraton (the host hotel) and the Marriott right next door, so you can stay close to the action and connect outside of sessions. Details will be sent in your welcome email. You’re encouraged to coordinate in the Circle community to share rooms and save costs.

What is the ticket refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable. They are transferable until April 30, 2026. This policy keeps the energy of the room committed and focused — and ensures every seat belongs to someone who truly wants to be there.

Will we be able to meet the speakers?

Many speakers include time for questions during or after their talks, and may mingle before/after sessions. VIP ticket-holders will also have access to an intimate Saturday dinner with speakers and fellow creators.

Hi, I’m Jesse James Carver ("The Unblocked Carver")

Wood carver, web designer, and founder of The Living Internet Alliance. I launched this convention to help creators reclaim their humanity and build profitable brands around it.

The desert quest that started it all

Last December I fled the Canadian winter and was living out of a tent in the Mojave Desert, carving blocks of cedar and chasing clarity. One night I joined a live call with the team at Kortex, the creator platform built by Dan Koe and his team. A few weeks later, Dan and I were standing together on a rocky viewpoint in Arizona talking about consciousness, creativity, and human relevance.

"Now that technical work and intelligence are on tap thanks to AI, what is left is taste, agency, and coherence." — Dan Koe

I came home knowing I had to build something alive. Something for creators who refuse to become mere products of the algorithm. That seed of an idea became the Creator Economy Convention: a weekend to rediscover what makes you human, align with your tribe, and build a powerful brand around your unique voice.

I'm looking forward to seeing you there.
