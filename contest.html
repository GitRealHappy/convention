The Concept

The contest would run alongside the 8-week Future-Proof Workshop Series (leading to the June 5–7 summit). The workshop series itself is free — anyone can attend any of the live calls at no cost. To enter the contest, participants would either hold a GA or VIP ticket (and be in the running for the in-person prizes) or enter without a ticket if they can't afford GA or can't make it to Vancouver (in which case they're in the running for 3 virtual tickets).

Contestants simply need to attend at least 4 out of 8 workshops (live or replay) and post about their experience. At the end of the series, a winner is selected randomly from all eligible entries using a random number generator.

The winner is announced on May 2nd, one week after the final workshop.

The Prize

If the winner has a GA ticket:

  • Free upgrade to VIP ($700 value — the difference between GA at $299 and VIP at $999)

If the winner already has a VIP ticket:

  • A free VIP ticket to bring a friend ($999 value). Their friend gets the full VIP experience alongside them.

If the winner doesn't have a ticket (e.g. can't afford GA or can't fly to Vancouver):

  • 3 virtual tickets — for themselves and two friends. All three can attend the summit virtually.

VIP includes: exclusive Saturday dinner with speakers, front-row seating, after-party ticket, and 2 years of Premium Living Internet University membership.

How Participants Enter

Anyone can join the workshop series for free and attend any live call at no cost. To enter the contest, participants must:

  1. Either hold a GA or VIP ticket to the Future-Proof Creator Summit (June 5–7, 2026) or be entering without a ticket (e.g. can't afford GA or can't fly to Vancouver — in that case they're in the running for 3 virtual tickets only).
  2. Attend at least 4 out of 8 workshops — either live or by watching the recorded replay.
  3. Post a reel on Instagram or a video on YouTube for each workshop attended, describing how they are applying what they learned.
  4. Tag #futureproofcreatorsummit and @living.internet.alliance in every post.

Eligibility would be verified through the tagged posts. I might also ask participants to submit a simple form at the end with links to their posts.

How the Winner Is Selected

At the end of the 8-week series, all eligible entries are compiled. A random number generator is used to select one winner from the pool of eligible participants.

Every eligible entry has an equal chance of winning — no judging, no voting, just luck. This keeps it simple and fair.

The winner is announced on May 2nd, one week after the final workshop.

What I'm Asking of You

The only commitment is your workshop. The contest runs alongside the series, but you don't need to do anything extra — the winner is selected randomly from eligible participants.

I'd welcome your feedback on whether this structure works or if you'd suggest doing it differently.
